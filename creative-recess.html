This challenge is about falling in love with the process. By showing up, you’ll rewire your brain to create without constant second guessing and you’ll rediscover the playfulness of making art.

Your work might become a song, poem, lyric, story, dance, letter, visual piece, or something totally unexpected. You’re encouraged to explore freely. Most folks gravitate toward words, music, or performance, but anything goes.
